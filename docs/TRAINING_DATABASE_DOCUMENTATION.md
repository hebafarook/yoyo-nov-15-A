### 1. SPEED EXERCISES

#### 30m Sprint Intervals (`sprint_intervals_30m`)
- **Category**: Speed
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Intensity**: Maximum
- **Equipment**: Cones, Stopwatch
- **Purpose**: Develops maximum running speed, acceleration, and neuromuscular coordination
- **Expected Outcome**: Improve 30m sprint time by 0.1-0.3 seconds over 4-6 weeks
- **Instructions**:
  1. Set up two cones 30 meters apart
  2. Start in 3-point stance (one hand down)
  3. Sprint at maximum effort to far cone
  4. Focus on arm drive and forward lean
  5. Walk back slowly for recovery
  6. Repeat for specified sets
- **Progression**:
  - Week 1-2: 4 sets, 90s rest, 95% intensity
  - Week 3-4: 5 sets, 90s rest, 100% intensity
  - Week 5-6: 6 sets, 120s rest, 100% intensity

### 2. AGILITY EXERCISES

#### Speed Ladder Agility Complex (`ladder_agility_drill`)
- **Category**: Agility
- **Duration**: 15 minutes
- **Intensity**: High
- **Equipment**: Speed Ladder
- **Purpose**: Improves foot speed, coordination, balance, and reactive agility
- **Expected Outcome**: 20% improvement in agility test times, better first touch control
- **Instructions**:
  1. Lay out speed ladder on flat ground
  2. Start at one end in athletic stance
  3. Pattern 1: Two feet in each box (10 boxes)
  4. Pattern 2: In-in-out-out pattern
  5. Pattern 3: Single leg hops (right, then left)
  6. Stay on balls of feet throughout
  7. Maintain quick, light ground contact
- **Progression**:
  - Week 1-2: 3 rounds, 3 patterns, 60s rest
  - Week 3-4: 4 rounds, 4 patterns, 45s rest
  - Week 5-6: 5 rounds, 5 patterns, 30s rest

### 3. TECHNICAL SKILL EXERCISES

#### Ball Mastery Cone Weaving (`ball_mastery_cone_weaving`)
- **Category**: Technical
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Intensity**: Medium
- **Equipment**: Cones, Soccer Ball
- **Purpose**: Develops close ball control, spatial awareness, tight space manipulation
- **Expected Outcome**: Improved ball control rating, increased 1v1 confidence
- **Instructions**:
  1. Set up 6 cones in straight line, 2 yards apart
  2. Start with ball at first cone
  3. Weave: inside right, inside left, outside right, outside left
  4. Keep ball within 1 yard throughout
  5. Accelerate through last two cones
  6. Turn around and repeat
  7. Keep head up and scan field
- **Progression**:
  - Week 1-2: 5 reps, 70% speed, unlimited touches
  - Week 3-4: 7 reps, 80% speed, 2 touches per cone
  - Week 5-6: 10 reps, 90% speed, 1 touch per cone

#### Passing Accuracy Through Gates (`passing_accuracy_gates`)
- **Category**: Technical
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Intensity**: Medium
- **Equipment**: Cones, Soccer Balls
- **Purpose**: Improves passing accuracy, weight of pass, finding teammates
- **Expected Outcome**: 10-15% increase in passing accuracy, improved assists
- **Instructions**:
  1. Set up 5 small gates (1 yard wide) at 10, 15, 20, 25, 30 yards
  2. Start with 15 balls at central position
  3. Pass through each gate using inside of foot
  4. Focus on proper body position
  5. Follow through towards target
  6. Retrieve and repeat from different angles
  7. Progress to 50% weak foot
- **Progression**:
  - Week 1-2: 10 successful passes per gate, both feet
  - Week 3-4: 15 successful passes, add movement
  - Week 5-6: 20 successful passes, under pressure

### 4. TACTICAL EXERCISES

#### 4v4+2 Positional Play (`small_sided_positioning`)
- **Category**: Tactical
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Intensity**: High
- **Equipment**: Cones, Soccer Balls, Bibs
- **Purpose**: Develops tactical awareness, positioning, decision-making under pressure
- **Expected Outcome**: Improved game intelligence, better positioning in match situations
- **Instructions**:
  1. Set up 20x20 yard grid with cones
  2. 4 attackers + 2 neutral players vs 4 defenders
  3. Objective: Keep possession for 6+ consecutive passes
  4. Neutrals always play with team in possession
  5. Defenders win ball = roles switch
  6. Focus on spacing, support angles, movement off ball
  7. Coach provides tactical cues during play
- **Progression**:
  - Week 1-2: 3-minute rounds, 2-touch limit
  - Week 3-4: 4-minute rounds, free play
  - Week 5-6: 5-minute rounds, add finishing zones

### 5. PHYSICAL CONDITIONING

#### Soccer-Specific Plyometric Circuit (`plyometric_jump_circuit`)
- **Category**: Physical
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Intensity**: High
- **Equipment**: Boxes, Cones, Hurdles
- **Purpose**: Develops explosive power for headers, tackles, and general athleticism
- **Expected Outcome**: 15-20% improvement in vertical jump, increased power output
- **Instructions**:
  1. Box jumps: 3 sets x 8 reps (24" box)
  2. Lateral bounds: 3 sets x 10 reps each side
  3. Single leg hops: 3 sets x 6 reps each leg
  4. Tuck jumps: 3 sets x 8 reps
  5. Broad jumps: 3 sets x 5 reps
  6. Rest 90 seconds between exercises
  7. Focus on quick ground contact and maximum effort
- **Progression**:
  - Week 1-2: 2 sets per exercise, 24" box
  - Week 3-4: 3 sets per exercise, 30" box
  - Week 5-6: 3 sets per exercise, 36" box, add weight vest

#### VO2 Max Shuttle Run Intervals (`vo2_max_shuttle_runs`)
- **Category**: Physical
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Intensity**: Maximum
- **Equipment**: Cones
- **Purpose**: Develops aerobic capacity and high-intensity running endurance
- **Expected Outcome**: Improved VO2 max by 5-10%, enhanced match endurance
- **Instructions**:
  1. Set up cones 20 meters apart
  2. Sprint to far cone and back (40m total)
  3. Perform at 90-95% max effort
  4. Active rest: light jog for 60 seconds
  5. Repeat for 8-12 intervals
  6. Monitor heart rate (should reach 85-95% max)
  7. Cool down with 5-minute jog
- **Progression**:
  - Week 1-2: 8 intervals, 60s rest
  - Week 3-4: 10 intervals, 45s rest
  - Week 5-6: 12 intervals, 30s rest

### 6. PSYCHOLOGICAL TRAINING

#### Pressure Decision Making Drill (`pressure_decision_making`)
- **Category**: Psychological
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Intensity**: High
- **Equipment**: Cones, Balls, Bibs
- **Purpose**: Improves decision-making under pressure and mental composure
- **Expected Outcome**: Faster, better decisions in match situations, reduced errors
- **Instructions**:
  1. Set up 3 passing lanes with defenders
  2. Player receives ball with back to goal
  3. Must turn and make decision within 2 seconds
  4. Options: Pass, dribble, or shoot
  5. Defender applies realistic pressure
  6. Coach calls out scenarios randomly
  7. Debrief after each rep on decision quality
- **Progression**:
  - Week 1-2: 1 defender, 3-second window
  - Week 3-4: 2 defenders, 2-second window
  - Week 5-6: 3 defenders, 1-second window, crowd noise

#### Match Situation Visualization (`visualization_mental_rehearsal`)
- **Category**: Psychological
- **Duration**: 15 minutes
- **Intensity**: Low (Mental)
- **Equipment**: Quiet space, Optional audio guidance
- **Purpose**: Enhances mental preparation, confidence, and performance under pressure
- **Expected Outcome**: Improved mental toughness, reduced anxiety, better focus
- **Instructions**:
  1. Find quiet space and sit/lie comfortably
  2. Close eyes and regulate breathing (4-7-8 pattern)
  3. Visualize specific match scenarios:
     - Receiving difficult pass under pressure
     - Scoring important goal
     - Making crucial defensive play
     - Staying calm after mistake
  4. Engage all senses: see, hear, feel the situation
  5. Visualize successful outcomes in detail
  6. Practice positive self-talk
  7. Repeat daily for best results
- **Progression**:
  - Week 1-2: 5 minutes, basic scenarios
  - Week 3-4: 10 minutes, complex scenarios
  - Week 5-6: 15 minutes, high-pressure scenarios

## üéì COACHING NOTES

### Best Practices:
1. **Warm-up**: Always include 10-minute dynamic warm-up
2. **Cool-down**: 5-10 minutes of light activity and stretching
3. **Hydration**: Frequent water breaks, especially in high-intensity sessions
4. **Form First**: Prioritize proper technique over speed/volume
5. **Recovery**: Adequate rest between sessions (24-48 hours for high-intensity)
6. **Feedback**: Provide specific, actionable coaching cues
7. **Adaptation**: Adjust based on player fatigue, injury, or rapid improvement

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:
- Skipping warm-up/cool-down
- Too much volume too soon
- Ignoring rest and recovery
- Practicing at game speed before mastering technique
- Not tracking progress
- Inconsistent training schedule
